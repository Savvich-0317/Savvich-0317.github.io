What is fumo?

Fumo are soft plush dolls created by the Japanese company GIFT.
They are small, very cute, with expressive eyes and a soft texture, making them popular among collectors and fans of cozy toys.
These dolls are often used to decorate rooms, for photo shoots, or as collectible items.
They can be found in online stores, but original Japanese versions can be quite expensive.
Mostly of presented here are not maded by GIFT which is can be categorized as counterfeit but it is actually ok.

If you need more information about fumos you you can check it out at fumo.website
